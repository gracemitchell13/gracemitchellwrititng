About

I'm a product, program, and grant manager with 20+ years of experience building and managing civic technology, preparing grant proposals, and delivering government digital services. I specialize in turning complex systems into tools that actually work for the people who use them.

Most recently, I served as Product Manager at Housing Navigator Massachusetts, a statewide affordable housing search platform serving 450K+ users annually. Before that, I was an IT Project Manager at the City of Boston Mayor's Office of Housing, where I oversaw the software system behind $38M+ in rental relief funds delivery. I hold a Master of Public Affairs from UT Austin's LBJ School, PMP and CNP credentials, and multiple AI certificates.

I serve as a board member of the Newton Fair and Affordable Housing Partnership and a strategic advisor for the Elkton Community Education Center.
