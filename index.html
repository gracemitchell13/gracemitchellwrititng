About

I'm a product and program manager with 20+ years of experience building civic technology, managing housing policy initiatives, and delivering government digital services. I specialize in turning complex systems into tools that actually work for the people who use them.

Most recently I served as Product Manager at Housing Navigator Massachusetts, a statewide platform serving 450K+ users annually, and as IT Project Manager at the City of Boston Mayor's Office of Housing, where I oversaw $38M+ in rental relief delivery. I hold a Master of Public Affairs from UT Austin's LBJ School, PMP, and CNP credentials.

I'm based in Newton, MA and serve as a board member of the Newton Fair and Affordable Housing Partnership.
